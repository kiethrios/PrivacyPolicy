Contact

If you have questions, feedback, or need help, email us at klproductgethelp@gmail.com.

When reporting an issue, please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened.

Common Questions

  • How do I create a vocabulary card? Open Capture, take a photo or choose an image, review the suggested Japanese word, then save the card.
  • Why is the camera not working? Make sure camera access is enabled in iOS Settings. If the device or simulator does not provide a live camera preview, try importing a photo instead.
  • Why was no subject detected? Try a clearer photo with one main object, better lighting, and less background clutter.
  • How do I review saved words? Open the Review tab to see saved cards and mark words as learned or mastered.
  • How do I delete my data? Delete saved cards in the app when deletion is available. Uninstalling the app removes local app data from your device; manage device backups separately if needed.
  • How do I manage purchases or subscriptions? If App Store purchases are available, manage subscriptions, cancellations, refunds, and restores through Apple and the App Store.

Privacy

Hibi Lens processes captures and saved vocabulary cards on your device. The app does not use ads, third-party analytics, cross-app tracking, or our own backend server for your captured images.

Common Questions

  • How do I remove ads? Upgrade to the lifetime plan in the app’s Paywall.
  • How do I restore purchases? Go to Settings > Membership > Restore Purchases.
  • How do I change language or currency? Settings > Language / Currency. Currency changes symbol only.
  • How do I delete my data? Uninstalling the app removes local data; manage device backups if needed.
